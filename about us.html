About Us

Our History

Founded in 2008 by Mrs.Kevin Silva, The City Hospital began with a vision to provide exceptional care in a single building. Over the decades, we have expanded our facilities and services, incorporating cutting-edge technology and specialized departments to meet the evolving needs of our patients. Significant milestones, such as as the introduction of our state-of-the-art imaging center in 2012, the expansion of our services with the opening of a comprehensive cancer center in 2015, and the launch of our community health outreach program in 2019, have marked our journey and strengthened our commitment to medical excellence. Today, The City Hospital stands as a pillar of health and hope, dedicated to delivering compassionate care and advancing healthcare through innovation, all while honoring the principles and values established by our founders.

Our Hospital

At The City Hospital, we are dedicated to delivering outstanding healthcare services with a focus on compassion, innovation, and excellence. Our state-of-the-art facility integrates advanced medical technology with a patient-centered approach, ensuring personalized and effective care for each individual. From routine check-ups and preventive care to complex surgeries and specialized treatments, our expert team of physicians, nurses, and support staff is committed to addressing every aspect of health and wellness. We emphasize a holistic approach, treating not just the illness but the whole person. Our comprehensive range of services includes cutting-edge medical interventions, wellness education, rehabilitation, and community outreach programs. We prioritize preventive care and patient education to empower individuals in managing their health proactively. Our hospital is designed to provide a supportive and healing environment where patients and their families are treated with dignity and respect. We foster a culture of empathy and collaboration, working closely with patients to understand their needs and preferences. Our team is dedicated to creating a comforting atmosphere that promotes both physical and emotional healing.

As a cornerstone of our community, we are deeply committed to enhancing the quality of life through continuous improvement and innovation. We strive to create an atmosphere of trust and comfort, where our patients receive exceptional care and support every step of the way. At The City Hospital, we are proud of our rich history and steadfast commitment to advancing healthcare and fostering a healthier, more vibrant community.

Our Services & Facilities
